About Wexham Medical Resources

Who We Are

Wexham Medical Resources, Inc. is a Philippine-based company specializing in the importation, marketing, and distribution of high-quality surgical products. We serve hospitals, clinics, and healthcare institutions across the country with a product portfolio that spans sutures, surgical mesh, hemostats, staplers, clip appliers, and minimally invasive surgery tools.

Our products are sourced from world-class manufacturing partners, including Lotus Surgicals, whose ISO-certified facilities ensure every item meets international quality and safety standards. We are committed to improving surgical outcomes for Filipino patients by making premium surgical supplies accessible and affordable.

Providing world-class surgical products to Filipino healthcare — that is our mission, our commitment, and our daily purpose.

Our Commitment

At Wexham, we believe that every surgeon deserves the best tools, and every patient deserves the safest care. That's why we work tirelessly to maintain our product quality, expand our range, and provide responsive support to the healthcare professionals who rely on us.

We are headquartered in Mandaluyong, Philippines, serving the entire country with a team of dedicated medical sales and customer care professionals.
